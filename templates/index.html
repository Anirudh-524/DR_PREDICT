📊 Model Overview

VisNet is a hybrid deep learning architecture combining Vision Transformers (ViT) with EfficientNet for robust diabetic retinopathy classification. The model achieves 97.5% accuracy on the validation dataset.

🏥 Classification Classes

No DR
No signs of diabetic retinopathy. Retina appears healthy with normal blood vessels.
Mild
Mild nonproliferative diabetic retinopathy. Microaneurysms present in the retina.
Moderate
Moderate nonproliferative diabetic retinopathy. More extensive vascular abnormalities.
Severe
Severe nonproliferative diabetic retinopathy. Extensive hemorrhages and venous abnormalities.
Proliferative DR
Proliferative diabetic retinopathy. New blood vessel growth and potential vision-threatening complications.
